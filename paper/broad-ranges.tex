\section*{Highlights}

\begin{itemize}
	\item uses open electricity system model PyPSA-Eur to explore near-optimal
	trade-offs
	\item wide range of options to design renewable power systems within 8\% of
	optimum
	\item many of these options are robust to uncertain technology cost
	developments
	\item few boundary conditions exist: some long-term storage and network
	expansion
	\item degrees of freedom can help policymakers to circumvent public
	acceptance issues
\end{itemize}
